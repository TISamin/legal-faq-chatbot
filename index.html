Step-by-Step Legal Guides

Detailed instructions for common legal procedures in Bangladesh

How to File a GD

Step-by-step guide to filing a General Diary at your local police station.

  1. Visit your nearest police station.
  2. Request the GD form from the duty officer.
  3. Provide your personal information and incident details clearly.
  4. Submit the form with your signature.
  5. Collect the GD reference number for future tracking.

How to File an FIR

Complete process for filing a First Information Report for criminal cases.

  1. Go to the police station where the crime occurred.
  2. Report the incident to the Officer-in-Charge or duty officer.
  3. Provide detailed information: time, place, people involved.
  4. Police will record the FIR and read it back to you.
  5. Sign the FIR after verification and collect the copy/reference.

Property Registration

Documentation and process for registering land or property in Bangladesh.

  1. Verify property ownership at the Land Office.
  2. Collect Non-Encumbrance Certificate (NEC).
  3. Pay land development tax at the local office.
  4. Prepare sale deed via licensed deed writer.
  5. Pay stamp duty, registration fees, and VAT.
  6. Submit documents to Sub-Registrar Office and give biometric.
  7. Collect your registered deed as proof of ownership.

Marriage Registration

Legal requirements and process for registering a marriage in Bangladesh.

Muslim Marriage (Nikah):
  1. Choose a registered Kazi (Marriage Registrar).
  2. Provide NID/Birth Certificate + 2 passport photos each.
  3. Ensure two adult witnesses are present.
  4. Fill the Nikah Nama and agree on Denmohor.
  5. Kazi registers and issues official Marriage Certificate.
Civil/Other Marriages:
  1. Apply at Marriage Registrar office with required documents.
  2. Registrar issues a 30-day public notice.
  3. After notice, marriage is registered with signatures + witnesses.

Trade License

How to obtain a trade license for your business in Bangladesh.

  1. Visit local City Corporation/Municipality office.
  2. Collect the Trade License application form.
  3. Attach NID copy, photos, TIN, rental deed/ownership proof.
  4. Submit form with required fees.
  5. Pay the license fee and sign register.
  6. Collect Trade License certificate (valid 1 year, renewable).

Passport Application

Complete guide to applying for a Bangladeshi passport.

  1. Visit passport.gov.bd and fill online form.
  2. Pay fees via A-Challan/Bank/Online.
  3. Book appointment at passport office.
  4. Bring application, payment slip, NID/Birth Certificate, old passport (if any).
  5. Complete biometric (photo + fingerprint).
  6. Track status online with delivery slip.
  7. Collect passport (Normal: ~15 days, Urgent: ~7 days).
