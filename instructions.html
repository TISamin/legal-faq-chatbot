Instructions

This section provides useful instructions on basic legal tasks in Bangladesh:

How to File an FIR

1. Visit the nearest police station.
2. Write down your complaint clearly with all facts.
3. Submit it to the duty officer.
4. Collect a copy of the FIR with the GD number.

How to Apply for Legal Aid

You can apply for legal aid from the National Legal Aid Services Organization (NLASO). Fill out the application form available at the District Legal Aid Office.
