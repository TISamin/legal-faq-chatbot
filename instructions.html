Detailed Legal & Civic Guides (Bangladesh)

How to File an FIR (First Information Report)

Where: Police Station in the jurisdiction of the crime.

Steps:

  1. Go to the local Police Station (Thana).
  2. Provide details: what happened, when, where, and who was involved.
  3. Submit written application or give oral statement (they must write it down).
  4. Ensure it is registered under Section 154 CrPC as FIR.
  5. Take a free certified copy of FIR for your record.

Note: Police cannot legally refuse to record an FIR.

How to File a GD (General Diary)

Purpose: Record non-criminal issues like lost documents, missing persons, threats.

  1. Visit nearest Police Station.
  2. Write down the event clearly (lost NID, harassment, etc.).
  3. Submit it at the Duty Officer desk.
  4. Receive a GD entry number as proof.
How to Apply for Bail

Hire a lawyer and apply to the Magistrate or Sessions Court depending on case type. Bail is discretionary, but in bailable offenses, it is a right.

How to File a Case in Court
  1. Consult a lawyer specialized in your matter (criminal, civil, family, etc.).
  2. Prepare plaint/petition with facts, laws, and relief sought.
  3. Submit in appropriate Court Registry.
  4. Pay court fees (varies by case type).
  5. Case will be numbered, notices issued to the opponent.
How to Get a National ID (NID)
  1. Visit NID Service Portal.
  2. Fill online registration form.
  3. Provide documents: Birth Certificate, SSC certificate, or Passport (for verification).
  4. Book appointment for biometric data (fingerprints, photo).
  5. Receive NID card after verification.
How to Get a Birth Certificate
  1. Apply through Union Parishad/City Corporation Birth Registration Office.
  2. Provide hospital birth slip or affidavit.
  3. Parents’ NID is required.
  4. Processing usually takes 7–10 working days.
How to Get a Passport
  1. Go to Online Passport Application.
  2. Fill out online form and pay fee via bank.
  3. Visit Passport Office with NID, Birth Certificate, and application receipt.
  4. Biometric capture and police verification.
  5. Collect e-passport within 15–30 days depending on service type.
How to Get a Trade License
  1. Visit City Corporation or Union Parishad office.
  2. Fill up prescribed trade license form.
  3. Submit owner’s NID, holding tax receipt, and application fee.
  4. License is usually issued in 2–7 working days.
How to Register a Marriage
  1. For Muslim marriage: Contact local Nikah Registrar (Kazi).
  2. Documents needed: Bride & Groom NID/Birth Certificate, guardian’s NID.
  3. Kazi fills in Nikahnama form and registers officially.
  4. Marriage certificate is issued immediately.
How to Register Land/Property
  1. Prepare deed of sale with lawyer/notary.
  2. Submit deed to Sub-Registrar office.
  3. Pay registration fee, stamp duty, VAT.
  4. Registrar officially records ownership transfer.
  5. Mutation at Land Office required to update records.
